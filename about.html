About Hansha

At Hansha, we understand the profound impact a mirror can have on the spaces we inhabit. It's not just a reflective surface; it's a portal to self-expression, an enhancer of light and beauty, and a statement of your unique style. As a leading mirror company, we take pride in curating a diverse collection of mirrors that not only reflect your image but also reflect your individuality.

Fun Fact !  Hansha is the japanese translation of Reflection

Our Story

At Hansha, our journey began in 1995, driven by a passion for craftsmanship and the desire to transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary reflections of beauty and style. Since our inception, we have been dedicated to crafting mirrors that are not just reflective surfaces but works of art in themselves.

  1. Craftsmanship Beyond Compare: We transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary places. We bring together time-honored techniques and cutting-edge technology to create mirrors that stand the test of time.

  2. Design Excellence and Diversity: Diversity is at the heart of our design philosophy. We offer a wide range of mirror styles, from classic and elegant to modern and avant-garde.

  3. Quality and Sustainability: Our materials are sourced sustainably, and our manufacturing processes are eco-friendly, reducing our carbon footprint.

  6. Exceptional Service: We believe in the power of customer satisfaction. Our dedicated team is here to guarantee timely delivery and professional installation services to ensure your mirror is a seamless addition to your space.
